# Prompt and Image Gallery

This document collects one shared base prompt template and its matching example images for later extension, replacement, and random combination.

## Base Prompt Template

> Vertical 9:16 modern Japanese anime-style illustration of a [CHARACTER TYPE], [HAIR COLOUR AND STYLE], [EYE COLOUR], wearing [CLOTHING DESCRIPTION].
>
> The character stands outdoors under a vivid bright blue summer sky with large soft white clouds. Low-angle fashion portrait composition, centered, three-quarter body to full-body view, head near the upper third of the image, looking slightly downward or to the side with a calm confident expression. Natural relaxed pose with subtle model-like charm, [POSE DESCRIPTION], elegant body posture.
>
> The character must feel mature, attractive, stylish and subtly sensual, with bold stylized anime proportions, beautiful body silhouette, clear waistline and confident fashion-model presence. Keep it tasteful and non-explicit, no pornography, no nudity.
>
> Art style: high-quality 2D Japanese anime illustration, soft painterly cel shading, clean but slightly sketchy natural linework, gentle watercolor-like color transitions, soft sunlight highlights, airy summer atmosphere, smooth skin rendering, delicate facial features, expressive eyes, refined fantasy-romance illustration feeling. Bright blue-and-white color palette, dreamy clouds, soft backlight, crisp focus, high detail, elegant composition.
>
> No text, no watermark, no logo, no border, no extra characters, no distorted hands, no bad anatomy, no childish appearance.
